Leo

July 23 - August 22 Fire Planet: Sun Fixed

Personality

Leo is the radiant heart of the zodiac. Ruled by the Sun, the only celestial body that generates its own light, it possesses a natural magnetism that attracts all eyes. Their presence fills a room with the same naturalness with which the Sun illuminates the sky. They don't seek attention: they simply generate it by existing.

Leo's generosity is legendary. When they love, they do so with all their heart and expect the same in return. They are the friend who throws the party, the leader who inspires the team, and the creator who puts their signature on everything they make. This need for personal expression is the engine that drives their life.

Their light shines in creativity, generosity, and the ability to make those around them feel special. Their shadow includes excessive pride, a need for constant admiration, and difficulty accepting criticism. Leo's path is learning that true greatness doesn't need external applause.

Element and Ruling Planet

As a fixed Fire sign, Leo represents the eternal flame: not Aries' initial burst nor Sagittarius' wandering spark, but the constant fire of the hearth that warms and illuminates. It is the creative fire that transforms raw material into a work of art.

The Sun, center of our solar system, grants Leo its central and radiant nature. Just as all planets orbit the Sun, Leo tends to be the center of their social group. The Sun rules identity, vitality, and creative will.

Love and Relationships

Leo loves with theatricality and generosity. They need a partner who genuinely admires them and doesn't compete for the spotlight. They are romantic, passionate, and tremendously loyal. When Leo chooses to love, they do so with a solar intensity that can warm or burn.

Best Compatibilities

  • Sagittarius — Adventure and optimism unite them in a vibrant relationship.
  • Aries — Two fires that mutually empower each other with passion.
  • Libra — Libra provides the harmony and admiration Leo needs.

Challenging Relationships

  • Taurus — Two fixed signs, both want things their way.
  • Scorpio — The intensity of both can create power struggles.

Career and Money

Leo shines in creative and leadership roles. They need a stage, whether literal or metaphorical, where they can express their unique vision.

  • Acting and performing arts — The stage is their natural habitat
  • Creative direction — Leads with artistic vision
  • Entrepreneurship — Their charisma attracts investors and clients
  • Politics — Public leadership is their element
  • Fashion design — Their aesthetic sense and drama create trends
  • Art education — Inspires creativity in others

Health and Wellness

Leo rules the heart, back, and spine. They are prone to heart problems, back tension, and exhaustion from physical overexertion.

  • Regular cardiovascular exercise to strengthen the heart
  • Take care of posture and strengthen the back
  • Rest adequately, don't overexert out of pride
  • Creative activities as a form of stress relief
