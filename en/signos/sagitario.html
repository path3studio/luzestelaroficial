Sagittarius

November 22 - December 21 Fire Planet: Jupiter Mutable

Personality

Sagittarius is the adventurous philosopher of the zodiac. Ruled by Jupiter, the planet of expansion and fortune, it possesses an unshakeable optimism and a thirst for knowledge that drives them to explore the world both physically and intellectually. Its symbol, the centaur archer, aims its arrow toward the sky: always toward something greater.

Freedom is Sagittarius' supreme value. They need space to explore, learn, and grow without restrictions. This need is not selfishness but a genuine expression of their expansive nature. When they feel trapped, whether in a relationship, a job, or a routine, their spirit withers.

Their light shines in contagious enthusiasm, unfiltered honesty, and the ability to find meaning in every experience. Their shadow includes irresponsibility, lack of tact, and a tendency to promise more than they can deliver. Sagittarius' path is learning that depth can be as enriching as breadth.

Element and Ruling Planet

As a mutable Fire sign, Sagittarius is the torch that illuminates the path toward the unknown. Their fire doesn't burn or build: it inspires and guides. It is the spark of inspiration that ignites curiosity and the desire to transcend the everyday.

Jupiter, the largest planet in the solar system, grants Sagittarius its generosity, optimism, and thirst for expansion. Jupiter rules philosophy, long-distance travel, and higher education, three areas where Sagittarius naturally flourishes.

Love and Relationships

Sagittarius loves with freedom and generosity. They need a companion who is also their adventure partner and who respects their independence. They don't tolerate jealousy or control. When they find someone who can run alongside them without trying to tie them down, they are a loyal and stimulating partner.

Best Compatibilities

  • Aries — Two adventurous fires that mutually empower each other.
  • Leo — The generosity and enthusiasm of both create a luminous relationship.
  • Aquarius — Both value freedom and progressive ideas.

Challenging Relationships

  • Virgo — Virgo's detail-orientation exasperates Sagittarius' expansive nature.
  • Pisces — Pisces' sensitivity clashes with Sagittarius' brutal frankness.

Career and Money

Sagittarius needs professions that offer variety, travel, or intellectual expansion. They suffocate in routine and flourish in the unpredictable.

  • University teaching — Sharing deep knowledge is their calling
  • Tourism and travel guiding — The world is their office
  • Philosophy and writing — Exploring ideas is their passion
  • International journalism — Combines travel, culture, and communication
  • International law — Large-scale justice motivates them
  • Motivational coaching — Their optimism is genuinely inspiring

Health and Wellness

Sagittarius rules the thighs, hips, and liver. They are prone to leg and hip injuries from excessive activity, and liver problems from dietary excesses.

  • Stretch properly before intense physical activities
  • Moderate alcohol consumption to protect the liver
  • Outdoor sports to channel their expansive energy
  • Take care of hip and knee joints
