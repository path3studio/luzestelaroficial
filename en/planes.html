Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between the portal and consultations?
The portal (Free or Premium) gives you ongoing access to interactive tools: calculators, daily horoscope, compatibility and weekly forecasts. Consultations are one-time professional reports (PDF + audio) generated specifically for you, delivered via email.
Do I need Premium to buy a consultation?
No. Consultations are independent from the portal. You can purchase an Essential or Complete Consultation without having a Premium subscription. They are separate products.
How long does my report take?
The Essential Consultation is delivered within 24-48 hours and the Complete Consultation within 24 hours. You will receive your professional PDF and audio directly to your email.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. You can activate or cancel Premium anytime from your dashboard. No commitments. Your access continues until the end of your billing period and your data is preserved.
