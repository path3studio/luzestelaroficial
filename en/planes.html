Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work?
When you activate Plus you get 7 full days at no cost with access to everything. You're not charged during the trial. Cancel anytime from your dashboard before day 7 and you won't be charged. If you don't cancel, your selected cadence (annual or monthly) begins.
What's the difference between Annual and Monthly?
The Annual cadence costs $29.99 per year (equivalent to $2.50/mo) — a 37% saving vs monthly. The Monthly cadence costs $3.99/mo and renews every 30 days. Both include the 7-day free trial and all Plus features. You can switch cadence later.
Can I cancel Plus anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard anytime, no questions asked. Access continues until the end of your billed period and your data is preserved if you decide to come back.
Do you offer a discount code?
Yes — during Stripe checkout you can enter the promo code ESTELAR!10 for 10% off your chosen plan.
