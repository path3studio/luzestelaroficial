Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need for my consultation?
Your full name, date of birth, exact time of birth (as precise as possible) and place of birth. The time is important to calculate your Ascendant and astrological houses.
How do I receive my report?
After payment, you will receive a professional PDF and an MP3 audio file directly to your email. The PDF includes charts, tables and your personalized interpretation.
Is this different from a daily horoscope?
Yes. A daily horoscope is general for everyone with your sign. The personalized consultation uses your exact date, time and place of birth to calculate unique planetary positions and generate an analysis that applies only to you.
Can I request a refund?
If you are not satisfied with your report, write to us at contacto@luzestelaroficial.com within the first 7 days. We will review your case and offer a solution.
What does the Stellar plan quarterly update include?
Every 3 months you will receive a new personalized forecast based on current transits over your birth chart, at no additional cost during the first year.
