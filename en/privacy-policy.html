Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Luz Estelar Oficial ("we", "our") operates the website luzestelaroficial.github.io and publishes content on third-party platforms including YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

Our primary service consists of publishing daily horoscope content. We collect minimal information:

  • Information you voluntarily provide: Comments, messages, and survey responses you send us through our social media platforms.
  • Usage data: Aggregate statistics provided by platforms (YouTube Analytics, Meta Insights, TikTok Analytics) that include views, likes, and general audience demographics. We do not have access to personally identifiable information through these analytics.
  • Subscription information: If you subscribe to premium services in the future, we may collect your name, email address, and date of birth (for your personalized birth chart).

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Publish and distribute daily horoscope content across our platforms.
  • Respond to comments and messages from our community.
  • Improve the quality and relevance of our content.
  • Generate internal statistics about our content performance.

4. Third-Party Platform Services

We use the official services of the platforms where we publish to manage our content and protect your experience. The use of each platform is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy (YouTube)
  • Meta Privacy Policy (Facebook and Instagram)
  • TikTok Privacy Policy

These services are used exclusively to publish content on our own official accounts. We do not access other users' data through them.

5. Storage and Security

API access tokens and credentials are stored securely in environment variables on our local servers. We do not store users' personal information in external databases.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information under our control, including encryption in transit and restricted access to credentials.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties. The only exception is when necessary to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our rights or those of others.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our static website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. The third-party platforms where we publish content (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok) may use their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
  • File a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority.

9. Minors

Our content is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The date of the last update is indicated at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
