Human Design & Gene Keys

Discover your Sun Gate and Gene Key

Human Design is a self-knowledge system that synthesizes four ancient traditions: the I Ching (64 hexagrams), astrology, the Tree of Life from Kabbalah, and the energy center (chakra) system. Each person has a unique map based on their date, time, and place of birth.

The Gene Keys by Richard Rudd expand each Gate into a spectrum of three frequencies: the Shadow (unconscious pattern), the Gift (unlocked potential), and the Siddhi (the highest expression).

What is the Sun Gate

At the moment of your birth, the Sun was in one of the 64 Gates of the Human Design mandala. This Sun Gate is the most dominant energy in your design — it represents your conscious identity and life purpose.

Each Gate corresponds to an I Ching hexagram and is divided into 6 lines that add additional nuance to your profile. The line indicates how you express your Gate's energy.

The Gene Key Spectrum

  • Shadow: The reactive, unconscious pattern. It is the lowest frequency of the Key and represents the challenge we must recognize and transform.
  • Gift: When we contemplate our Shadow with honesty, it transmutes into our natural Gift — a talent that benefits both ourselves and those around us.
  • Siddhi: The highest and rarest expression. It is a state of consciousness that transcends duality and represents the spiritual fulfillment of each Key.

The Four Pillars of Human Design

  • I Ching: The 64 hexagrams provide the 64 Gates of the BodyGraph, each with 6 lines.
  • Astrology: The position of the planets at the moment of birth activates specific Gates in your design.
  • Kabbalah: The Tree of Life connects the 9 Centers of the BodyGraph through 36 channels.
  • Chakras: The 9 Centers in Human Design are an evolution of the Hindu 7-chakra system, each with biological and energetic functions.
