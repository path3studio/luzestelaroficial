Compatibility

🔥 The 4 Elements

In astrology, the 12 signs are grouped into four elements that determine basic temperament and the way of interacting with the world. Elemental compatibility is one of the pillars of partnership analysis.

🔥 Fire — Aries, Leo, Sagittarius

Passion, action, and drive. Fire signs need constant movement and stimulation. Together they ignite each other, but can burn out. With Air they expand; with Earth or Water they clash unless balance is found.

🌍 Earth — Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn

Stability, pragmatism, and sensuality. They connect deeply with one another. With Water they form nurturing, lasting bonds. Fire agitates them and Air destabilizes them, though it can bring perspective.

💨 Air — Gemini, Libra, Aquarius

Intellect, communication, and sociability. Among themselves ideas flow endlessly. With Fire they create dynamism and adventure. With Earth and Water they need conscious effort to find common ground.

🌊 Water — Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces

Emotion, intuition, and depth. They understand each other without words. With Earth they build solid relationships. Fire evaporates them if not careful, and Air can leave them feeling misunderstood.

🔄 The 3 Modalities

Modalities describe how each sign approaches action and change. Two signs of the same modality compete for control; different modalities bring balance.

Cardinal — Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn

The initiators of the zodiac. They love starting projects and leading. Two cardinals together may clash over command, but if they negotiate roles, their combined energy is unstoppable.

Fixed — Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius

The stabilizers. Persevering and determined, but also stubborn. A fixed-sign couple is unbreakable if they share a vision, but inflexibility can create blockages.

Mutable — Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces

The adaptable ones. Flexible and versatile, but sometimes indecisive. Together they flow with ease, though they may lack structure. With a cardinal or fixed sign they find the anchor they need.

☯︎ Polarities

Each sign has an energetic polarity: Masculine (active, extroverted) or Feminine (receptive, introverted). Pairs of opposite polarities generate magnetic attraction; those of the same polarity share a natural rhythm.

  • Masculine (Yang) — Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius. Projective energy, directed outward.
  • Feminine (Yin) — Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces. Receptive energy, directed inward.
  • Opposites attract — The Yin-Yang tension creates spark and complementarity, but requires mutual understanding.
  • Like understands like — They share rhythm and communication style, though stimulating contrasts may be lacking.

🌑 Beyond the Sun

The Sun sign is just the entry point. True compatibility between two people depends on multiple factors in the birth chart:

  • Venus — How you love and what you need in a relationship. Venus compatibility between two charts reveals real romantic harmony.
  • Moon — Your deep emotional needs. A good lunar connection indicates that both feel emotionally safe together.
  • Mars — Desire, passion, and conflict style. The Mars dynamic between two charts shows physical chemistry and how differences are resolved.
  • Ascendant — The first impression and how you present yourself. Aspects between Ascendants determine initial attraction.
  • Full synastry — Professional analysis cross-references all planets in both charts to offer a complete picture, not just the Sun sign.
