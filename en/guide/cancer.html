Complete Guide to Cancer

Cancer is the fourth sign of the zodiac and is represented by the symbol of the crab. People born between June 21st and July 22nd are considered Cancers. As a water sign, Cancer is deeply connected to the emotional realm and is known for its strong intuition and nurturing nature. The planet associated with Cancer is the Moon, which is also the ruler of emotions, making Cancers highly attuned to the feelings of those around them. In terms of modality, Cancer is a cardinal sign, which means that they are naturally inclined to take action and initiate new projects, although they may also struggle with indecision at times.

The Cancer sign is often associated with the concept of home and family, and those born under this sign tend to be very protective of their loved ones. They are also known for their strong connection to their roots and their heritage, and they often have a deep appreciation for tradition and history. Cancers are highly empathetic and are naturally drawn to careers in healthcare, education, and social work, where they can use their nurturing skills to help others. Overall, Cancers are complex and multifaceted individuals with a rich inner life and a strong desire to connect with others on a deep and meaningful level.

As a water sign, Cancer is also associated with the subconscious mind and the world of dreams. Cancers are often highly intuitive and may have a strong connection to their own subconscious, which can guide them in making decisions and navigating the challenges of life. However, this connection to the subconscious can also make Cancers more prone to mood swings and emotional turmoil, as they are highly sensitive to the emotions of those around them. Despite these challenges, Cancers are known for their resilience and their ability to adapt to changing circumstances, and they are often highly successful in their personal and professional lives.
